When did I start writing? To tell you the truth, I don't know. It seems that this ability has always been with everyone since they were born, especially for students. This ability has been continuously cultivated and guided by teachers and parents from kindergarten to university. This is undoubtedly to lay a good foundation for everyone's literary literacy and pave different roads for people's future ways of ideological expression. The length and width of the road, in the final analysis, depends on how people accept the knowledge obtained from different teachers and seemingly boring books. For me, writing has become a part of my life. Sharing my ideas and opinions with others and getting affirmation or refutation makes me full of endless sense of achievement as well as enjoyable. For me, writing is not only a way to express my feelings, but also gives me confidence and encouragement when I need help most. This is one of the reasons why I insist on writing. Expression, this is undoubtedly an embarrassing and inferior behavior for me as a child. Introverted by nature, I can't imagine talking to others. I often think, maybe I should take the initiative to reveal my heart to others? But I'm afraid. I'm afraid.
